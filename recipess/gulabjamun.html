Gulab Jamun


Description


Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian dessert made of deep-fried milk-solid balls soaked in sweet, fragrant syrup flavored with cardamom. Soft and spongy, it's a festive treat enjoyed warm or at room temperature.

    Ingredients

  • Milk Powder
  • All-purpose flour
  • Ghee
  • Milk
  • Baking Soda
  • Oil/ghee for frying
      • For syrup:
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom Pods

    Steps

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Add milk to make a soft dough.
  3. Roll into small balls.
  4. Fry in oil until golden.
  5. Boil sugar, water, and cardamom for syrup.
  6. Soak fried balls in syrup.
  7. Serve warm!
